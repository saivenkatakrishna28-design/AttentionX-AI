DEMO VIDEO SCRIPT - ATTENTIONX AI

1. Introduction
Hello everyone, this is our project AttentionX AI, an automated content repurposing engine designed for mentors, educators, and creators.

2. Problem Statement
Creators often produce long-form content such as lectures, podcasts, and workshops. But modern audiences prefer short-form content. Valuable moments often stay hidden inside 30 to 60 minute videos. Extracting those moments manually takes a lot of time.

3. Our Solution
AttentionX AI solves this by automatically:
- transcribing the video
- identifying emotional and high-impact moments
- creating short-form clips
- generating captions and hooks for social media

4. Show the Interface
Here we have the Streamlit web app. The user uploads a long-form video and clicks Generate Shorts.

5. Explain the Pipeline
In the backend, we first transcribe the video using Whisper.
Then we analyze audio intensity and transcript quality.
We rank the best segments using a hybrid highlight score.
Finally, we export short clips with subtitles and vertical formatting support.

6. Show Output
Now we can see the top-ranked highlights, their timestamps, hook suggestions, and exported subtitle files.
These clips are ready for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

7. Innovation
Our innovation lies in combining multiple signals instead of using only timestamps or transcript keywords.
This gives better quality highlight detection and produces more engaging short-form content.

8. Impact
This can help educators, mentors, podcasters, and creators turn one long session into multiple shareable pieces of content, improving reach and audience engagement.

9. Closing
Thank you for watching. This is AttentionX AI.
